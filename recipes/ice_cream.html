Ice Cream

The finished, delicious product

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Corn Starch
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil milk, cream, suagr with very precise timing
  2. Remove from heat, stir in corn starch
  3. Pour over cream cheese and mix
  4. Cool it all down
  5. Put it in the ice cream maker
